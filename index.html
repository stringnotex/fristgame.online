ABOUT THE GAME

Monster Survivors is a thrilling roguelike survival game where you face wave after wave of bizarre creatures in a dystopian world. What begins as a simple fight for survival quickly evolves into an intense battle of strategy and skill as you unlock powerful weapons and abilities.

Each run is unique, with procedurally generated enemy patterns and random power-up combinations that ensure no two games are ever the same. As time passes, the difficulty ramps up, challenging even the most skilled players to push their limits and develop new strategies.

Inspired by classic roguelike games but infused with modern mechanics, Monster Survivors offers a perfect balance of accessibility and depth. The game features simple controls that anyone can pick up, but mastering the intricate systems of weapon combos and enemy patterns will take dedication and skill.

Pro Tips

Never Stop Moving

Standing still is a death sentence. Keep moving to avoid enemy attacks and create space.

Prioritize Synergistic Upgrades

Look for upgrades that complement each other rather than picking random abilities.

Collect XP Gems

Don't forget to collect experience gems from defeated enemies to level up faster.

Balance Your Build

A mix of offensive and defensive upgrades will serve you better than focusing solely on damage.
